Rajasthan — Forged in Fire

Overview

For centuries, travelling blacksmiths maintained tools for agrarian communities. Mobility and metalwork remain core to identity, while schooling, land rights, and housing shape everyday negotiations.

Work & Craft

Bellows, portable hearths, and recycled iron. Blades, sickles, and domestic tools are forged and bartered.

Everyday Life

Seasonal camps along roadsides; kin networks coordinate routes, marriages, and apprentice training.
